# P&K Forever - v0: The Original Text Adventure

> **Created:** April 21, 2022 (First Anniversary)
> **Technology:** HTML5 + Vanilla JavaScript (text-engine)
> **Status:** 🔒 **PRESERVED - DO NOT MODIFY**

## About This Version

This is the original "How We Met" text adventure game, created on the first anniversary of the day I met Anastasia at Geula Beach in Tel Aviv (April 21, 2021).

This version is preserved exactly as it was - a sacred artifact of that moment in time when GitHub Copilot helped bring this vision to life.

## The Story

The game tells the story of Phoenix (P) and K (Ehecatl) - how they met on a Tel Aviv beach, their first conversations about dreams and travel, and a flash-forward to an imagined future together in Japan and beyond.

It's a text adventure where:
- Every choice matters
- Items in the game unlock real-world Easter eggs
- The narrative blends whimsy with deep personal meaning
- The abstract (Phoenix) meets the grounded (K)

## Playing the Game

Open `index.html` in a web browser. That's it.

Commands:
- `LOOK` - Examine your surroundings
- `TALK TO [character]` - Have conversations
- `TAKE [item]` - Pick up items
- `USE [item]` - Use items in your inventory
- `GO [direction]` - Move between locations
- `FLY TO [location]` - Unlocked through gameplay
- `INV` - Check your inventory
- `SAVE` / `LOAD` - Save your progress

## Technical Details

- **Engine:** Custom text-engine (no dependencies)
- **Game Data:** `game-disks/p-n-k-forever.js`
- **Styling:** Retro terminal aesthetic (`styles/retro.css`)
- **ASCII Art:** Hand-crafted for each location
- **Easter Eggs:** IFTTT webhooks trigger real-world surprises
