# The Story of P&K Forever

> *A text adventure about two souls meeting on a beach in Tel Aviv*
>
> **Date:** April 21, 2021 (Friday)
> **Place:** Geula Beach, Tel Aviv, Israel
> **Moment:** When everything changed

---

## The Premise

This is the gamified story of how Phoenix and Anastasia met - the first day, the first conversation, and a flash-forward to an imagined future that didn't exist yet but felt inevitable.

**Phoenix (P):** A curious "birdcat" - abstract, whimsical, talented, always seeking the next adventure. Sometimes a bird, sometimes a cat, depending on how you look or her mood. She can fly when she believes in it enough.

**K (Ehecatl):** A peacockdog shiba riding a Brompton bicycle, wearing colorful feathers. More grounded, kind, a bit of a loner but pleasant. The one Phoenix never expected to find.

---

## Act I: How We Met
### The Beach (April 21, 2021)

#### Scene 1: Phoenix's Favorite Beach Slushy Place

Phoenix is at her favorite beach restaurant, gulping an açai slushy 🥤, waiting for the winds to calm. She's contemplating her next business endeavor - she's talented, she has skills, but what's next?

The summer sun beats down. It's hot. Too hot to move.

**Key Moments:**
- Phoenix needs to cool down with her slushy before she can leave
- Three gulps to go
- The heat is unbearable until that cold refreshment hits

#### Scene 2: The Beach Encounter

Then she appears from nowhere - not Phoenix. *Anastasia* appears.

Wait, no. Phoenix is the one already there. Then *K* appears.

A dog on a bicycle. Not just any dog - a peacockdog 🐕🦚 wearing colorful feathers. A shiba. Riding a Brompton folding bicycle.

Phoenix had never seen anything like this. Fascinated, she decided to approach.

**The First Conversation:**

Phoenix walks up. "What's your **NAME**?"

*"My name is **K.** Actually it's 'Ehecatl' but everybody calls me **K.**"*

Questions tumble out:
- "Your **BICYCLE** is cool, what is it?"
  - *"It's a Brompton, it's a folding bicycle"*

- "I've a **BUSINESS** question, do you mind?"
  - *"Not at all, ask away"*

- "I'm an **ARTIST**. I thought to start a dog portrait business. Since you're a dog, I thought you'd know a thing or two about it."
  - *"I'm not sure I'm up to it, but I've some business experience myself. I know various dog association people. I'll be glad to help out."*

A shekel glints in the sand. Phoenix picks it up. Flip it - heads or tails? The universe deciding something.

#### Scene 3: Sunset Walk to Jaffa

The sun starts to set. They've been talking for hours, it feels like.

"The sun is going down but I think we have much to talk about. Want to **CONTINUE** our conversation to the sunset?"

*"Sure, I've never met someone like you. I would love that. Let's walk beside the sunset to Jaffa - it's **SOUTH** of here."*

They walk together along the beach as the sky turns orange and purple.

**Deeper Conversations:**

Phoenix learns about K:
- **Favorite food?** Fruits & vegetables 🍓🥦
  - Phoenix loves **MANGO** 🥭 - eats them all the time

- **Favorite hot beverage?** Coffee 🍵
  - Phoenix is a **TEA** 🫖 person - with herbs: Mint & Lemon verbena, no sugar

- **A sweet you can't resist?** Ice cream 🍨
  - Phoenix: **CHOCOLATE** 🍫 - all kinds, especially dark chocolate
  - *"TIP: Look for a drawer, its number is four"* (an Easter egg, a hint for the real world)

- **Do you want to TRAVEL the world?**
  - *"I love to travel, I've traveled a lot. I want to be a digital nomad in the future."*

- **What's a digital NOMAD?**
  - *"A person who lives all over the world, traveling & working remotely."*

The biggest question: **"Do you want to travel with me around the WORLD?"**

*"I would love to travel with you. Where do you think we should head first?"*

Options swirl in conversation:
- Germany? 🇩🇪 *"I hate German people. Did I tell you about my German teacher?"*
- China? 🇨🇳 *"Looking for something more western..."*
- France? 🇫🇷 *"I hate French people"*
- Italy? 🇮🇹 *"Love their food 🍝 but don't see myself living there"*
- USA? 🇺🇸 *"YES! Let's go right now! USA! USA! ...wait, in second thought, not right now"*
- India? 🇮🇳 *"Indians are sticky, love their food though 🙈"*

Then Phoenix suggests: **"JAPAN** 🇯🇵"

*"I love Asian culture. The food, the people, their style. My ancestors are from Japan."*

It's decided.

**"How can we FLY to Japan ✈️?"**

*"Maybe we should take a plane?"*

Phoenix realizes something: *"I know! I can take us there. I can fly ✈️"*

**The FLY command unlocks.** Phoenix's secret power, revealed.

## Act II: The Flash-Forward
### Three Years Later...

#### Scene 4: Jaffa Apartment

They arrive at Phoenix's flat in Jaffa. It's full of memories and items from past travels.

K curls up in the living room bed. They sleep together until morning 💤

Phoenix wakes to the sound of Jaffa's streets. K is still snoring 😪

In the street outside, Phoenix finds her old kite equipment. A memory, a hobby, a piece of who she is.

**Easter Egg Unlocked:**
- Kite discount code: `PNK-n3zk7MAMBG-GIFT`
- (Real-world IFTTT trigger: `pnk_kite`)

#### Scene 5: Flying to Japan

Phoenix uses her power. They fly together to Kyoto 🏯

**Time skip: 3 years have passed...**

They land outside their Kyoto apartment. They live here now. Together.

#### Scene 6: Kyoto Apartment

The apartment smells wonderful - something's cooking in the **KITCHEN**.

Phoenix needs to change out of her dirty shoes. She finds her Uwabaki (house slippers) and her Tiger Zodiac Sign 🐅

She enters the kitchen.

#### Scene 7: The Kitchen - The Heart of Everything

K is cooking dim sum - famous Japanese dumplings.

**The Conversation That Matters:**

"What're you **MAKING**? 🍜"
*"I'm making dim sum."*

Phoenix finds her fancy chopsticks. Now she can properly eat the dumplings.

"What's the deal with the **TIGER** sign? 🐅📖"
*"This is your Zodiac sign - the Tiger."*

"What is your **ZODIAC** sign?"
*"I'm Snake 🐍"*

**Tiger and Snake. Protector and guide. Two creatures that make each other stronger.**

"How will our **FUTURE** be like?"

*"Our relationship will grow only stronger & stronger with the years. We will continue to challenge each other & make each other stronger - better together as a whole.*

*You're my protector & I'm yours. Stronger together, we're one. To eternity.*

*I've prepared a small gift for you. It's my **ZODIAC** sign - the **SNAKE** - to always protect you and help you be strong."*

K pulls out a beautiful silver double necklace with an **Infinity Ouroboros** amulet connected with a kite connector.

The snake eating its tail - infinity, eternity, the cycle that never ends. Two parts of one whole.

"Here, put this on your neck."

Phoenix uses it. Wears it. 🧡🖤🧡🖤

**"I think it's time to fly back to TLV. Let's get out of here together & fly."**

**Easter Egg Unlocked:**
- (Real-world IFTTT trigger: `pnk_fly`)

#### Scene 8: Home

They fly back to Tel Aviv. Their home.

**TLV Apartment. Israel. ✡️**

*And they lived happily ever after.*

## Themes

1. **The Abstract and the Grounded:** Phoenix is whimsical, hard to define. K is real, present, grounded. Together they balance.

2. **Discovery Through Conversation:** The game is about talking, learning, revealing. Each dialogue choice uncovers another layer.

3. **Powers Unlocked Through Connection:** Phoenix can't fly at first. She learns she can fly when she has someone to fly with.

4. **Real World Bleeds Into Game:** The Easter eggs aren't just in-game rewards - they trigger real-world surprises through IFTTT webhooks.

5. **The First Day Contains Everything:** How you meet someone tells you everything about how you'll love them. This first day - the questions, the slushy, the walk, the dreams of Japan - it was all there from the start.

6. **Time is Fluid:** The game jumps from Day 1 to Year 3, because when you know, you know. The future hasn't happened yet but it's already real.

## Technical Magic: The Gamification of Reality

| In-Game Event | Real-World Trigger | Meaning |
|---------------|-------------------|---------|
| Phoenix says she loves MANGO | `pnk_mango` webhook | Perhaps a mango appears in real life |
| Phoenix shares she drinks TEA | `pnk_drink` webhook | Maybe tea is prepared |
| Phoenix reveals she loves CHOCOLATE | `pnk_chocolate` webhook | Drawer #4 has something sweet |
| Phoenix finds KITE equipment | `pnk_kite` webhook | Discount code unlocked |
| Phoenix says "I LOVE you" | `pnk_love` webhook | Something beautiful happens |
| Phoenix and K FLY together | `pnk_fly` webhook | The ultimate trigger |

The game is meant to be played by Anastasia. As she discovers things in the game, she discovers things in real life.

## The Story Behind the Story

This game was created on April 21, 2022 - exactly one year after the day described in Act I actually happened.

At one year, the creator still felt they could never fully comprehend Anastasia - how unique she is, how lucky they were to know such a woman and have a romantic relationship with her.

This was their way of creating something unique. A love letter in code.

"This is what I know how to do. I know how to build software. I always wanted to make games because that brings me joy and makes my connection with computers more meaningful. And she gave me back the belief in myself that I can maybe someday make a game."

So they made this game.

With the help of GitHub Copilot (the early days of AI assistance), they brought this vision to life.

Now, years later, with Claude Code, we honor that original vision and plan what comes next.

---

*"Even a year knowing Anastasia, I understood how unique she is, and how something that I could never really, fully comprehend."*

This game is that understanding, gamified.
